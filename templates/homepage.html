How to Claim Donations (for NGOs)

  1. Onboarding & Verification: Register your NGO. Admin will verify and approve.
  2. Donation Publishing: Donors publish available items.
  3. Request: NGOs browse and request donations.
  4. Approval: Donor approves and status becomes “Claimed”.
  • Expiry: If the product is not requested before the expiry date, the product will not be available for donation.
