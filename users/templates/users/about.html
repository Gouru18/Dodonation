About DoDonation

"Our project is a web platform, named Dodonation which connects donors (individuals/shops that have excess items like food and clothing items) to vetted non-governmental organizations (NGOs) in their local area." DoDonation is a community-powered platform that connects generous individuals with NGOs in need. Our mission is to reduce waste and promote social good by making it easy to donate usable items like clothes, books, furniture, and electronics.
